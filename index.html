About Me

My name is Isabel. Since I was a little girl I was obsessed with sports. My favorite sport since I could remember was basketball. Nothing made me more happy than going outside and throwing a ball at the hoop and learning new tricks. Since then I've lost some of the moves I've learned throughout the years, but I still love basketball and all sports really. The top three sports I loved to play were soccer, volleyball, and basketball. Whenever I'd play volleyball my grandmother would make fun at how much I threw myself for the ball. In all honesty, that was one of my most favorite part of volleyball. Going in for a save and being able to continue a play when it seemed it was almost difficult if not impossible to happen. I saved a play only once, but it was one of the most exciting feelings I'd ever experienced.
Speaking on another subject, I love dogs. I especially love my two dogs Oliver and Mia. They can be a a handful sometimes but they are more hilarious than difficult to deal with.
